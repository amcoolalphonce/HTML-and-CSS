Will Alphonce Make it to 3rd Year

May 29th 2022 4 Min read

Alphonce Omondi is still unsure whether he will make it to third year. After the trying and testing that the second year brought, from Physical Electronics introduction to Eleectrical Circuit Theory, and the good or better Fluid Mechanics lecturer. "Its been a tough year. Professors and the University were on a mission to kill us!..." He laments.

Nevertheless, we have had an amazing moment of our lives. The numerous church activities that I have engaged on have broughtme some sense of purpose. I met good friends and the burden has been lifted. Who knew i could meet Wilstan who would guide me on Python and challenge? And Sherry? Magoma?...

I only hope it gets better with time. I'll be stronger during thid fourth semsester, and i know that i will bbounce back next academic year

Having made it out, I can only sy that,"Its been a hell of a year Thank God we made it out"

Alphonce Omondi
